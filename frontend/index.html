Public Supply Transparency Dashboard

ReliefSync maintains a public-facing accountability page so that communities, media, and civil society can independently verify that evacuation centers are properly stocked during a disaster. All supply levels and AI forecasts shown there are sourced directly from the same data used by DSWD relief officers.

  • Live stock levels per evacuation center
  • AI-predicted 72-hour demand per supply type
  • Overall supply health status (CRITICAL / WARNING / STABLE)
  • Works offline — shows last known data if internet drops
  • No login required — open to anyone
